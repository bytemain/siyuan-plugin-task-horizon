## Design Context

### Users
Task Horizon is used by SiYuan note users who manage a high volume of tasks inside documents and need to review, edit, analyze, and schedule them without leaving their current workflow.

The AI workspace is a high-frequency utility surface. Users come here to ask for next steps, analyze task quality, generate summaries, and turn task context into concrete schedules quickly.

### Brand Personality
Professional, restrained, dependable.

The interface should feel like a native power-tool enhancement to SiYuan rather than a flashy standalone product. It should build confidence, reduce visual noise, and make dense information easier to scan.

### Aesthetic Direction
Professional and restrained, with a native-enhanced desktop feel.

Favor clear hierarchy, compact but readable spacing, subtle emphasis, and light motion. Preserve compatibility with SiYuan theme variables and overall visual language. Avoid decorative flair, loud gradients, overly branded surfaces, or visually heavy effects that compete with the content.

### Design Principles
1. Respect the host: extend SiYuan's native UI language instead of replacing it.
2. Make hierarchy obvious: primary actions, active state, results, and supporting context should be visually distinct at a glance.
3. Keep density controlled: compact layouts are fine, but not at the cost of scanability or touch targets.
4. Use motion sparingly: transitions should clarify state changes, not draw attention to themselves.
5. Reduce ornamental styling: emphasis should come from structure, contrast, and spacing before color or effects.
